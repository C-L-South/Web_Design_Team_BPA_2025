988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (U.S.)

Available 24/7, this free and confidential service provides immediate support for people in emotional distress or suicidal crisis. Compassionate counselors are ready to listen and help.

Crisis Text Line (U.S., U.K., Canada, Ireland, South Africa)

A free, 24/7 text-based counseling service. Individuals can text HOME to 741741 (U.S.), 686868 (Canada), or 85258 (U.K.) to reach a trained crisis counselor. Ideal for people uncomfortable with phone calls.

SAMHSA National Helpline (U.S.)

The Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration provides a 24/7, free, confidential helpline for mental health and substance use disorders. They offer referrals to local treatment providers, support groups, and community resources. Their number is 1-800-662-HELP (4357)

Lifeline (Australia)

A free, 24/7 crisis-support service for anyone experiencing emotional distress, suicidal thoughts, or loneliness. They offer crisis counseling by phone, text, and online chat. Their number is 13 11 14, and you can text and chat through their website.
