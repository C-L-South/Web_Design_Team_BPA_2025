988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Available 24/7, this free and confidential service provides immediate support for people in emotional distress or suicidal crisis. Compassionate counselors are ready to listen and help.

Teen Line

Teen Line is a confidential support service for teenagers and young adults who want to talk about stress, anxiety, relationships, school pressure, or anything else on their mind. It connects youth with trained listeners who understand what it’s like to be a teen and can offer support without judgment.

SAMHSA National Helpline

The Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration provides a 24/7, free, confidential helpline for mental health and substance use disorders. They offer referrals to local treatment providers, support groups, and community resources. Their number is 1-800-662-HELP (4357)

Military OneSource

Military OneSource provides free, confidential support for service members, veterans, and their families. It offers help with mental health concerns, stress management, family challenges, deployment issues, and everyday life problems through counseling, resources, and referrals.
