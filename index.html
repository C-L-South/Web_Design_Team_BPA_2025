About Us

Our Team

We're a passionate team including Cody Li and Swadhin Nepal who built Mindscape with a purpose to make mental health awareness more open and accessible for everyone. We built this website from the ground up with over 7,000+ lines of code and over 100+ hours of development.

We created a website where people can share mental health expriences, book appointments, and have access to information and resources about mental health. This website was designed destigmatize mental health issues, providing resources and support to all.

Through Mindscape, we hope to inspire mental health awareness, break the stigma surrounding mental health, and give access to valuable tools to help individuals on their journey to well-being.
