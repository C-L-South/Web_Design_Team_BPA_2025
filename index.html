About Us

Our Team

We're a small but passionate team including Cody Li, Swadhin Nepal, and Nihar Dodla who built Mindscape with a purpose to make mental health awareness more open, relatable, and accessible for everyone.

We created a website where people can share mental health expriences, book appointments, and have access to information and resources about mental health. This website was thoughtfully designed in order to achieve a more empathetic community for all.

Through Mindscape, we hope to inspire honest conversations, spread awareness, and encourage small daily actions that nurture emotional strength, balance, and connection.
